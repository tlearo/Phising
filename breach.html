Data Breach Investigation

Triangulate suspicious login activity, track lateral movement, and pin down the compromised account.

Investigation Brief

The SOC escalated an alert after unusual access patterns hit your HR system. You have a compressed log bundle with authentication, VPN, and file access events. Your job is to reconstruct the incident timeline.

  • Identify which account was used as the initial access vector.
  • Note the timestamp and origin IP of the first malicious login.
  • Flag the exfiltration step—service + resource touched.

Record your findings in the team notebook. Only submit answers when the crew agrees on the full story—points are deducted for guesses.

Artifacts Provided

  1. vpn.csv — live VPN session metadata (IP, user, duration, MFA method).
  2. audit.json — application audit entries (actor, action, object, status).
  3. auth.log — condensed identity provider events (success/failure, device fingerprint).

Work through the artifacts as a team, capture the anomalies you spot, and share back with the facilitator to score this bonus round.
