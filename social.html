Social Engineering Scenario

Decode the persuader’s playbook and craft a response that protects your organisation without escalating the situation.

Scenario Playback

You will receive a transcript between a supposed supplier representative and one of your finance analysts. The attacker layers urgency plus authority to convince staff to reset multifactor authentication.

  • List every social engineering lever you spot (authority, scarcity, familiarity, etc.).
  • Draft a short, policy-aligned response that ends the conversation safely.
  • Recommend a follow-up control (awareness, process, or technical) that would stop the next attempt earlier.

Deliverables

Capture findings in the team report and flag them for your facilitator to validate. Bonus credit is awarded for bringing two mitigations—one people-focused and one technical.
