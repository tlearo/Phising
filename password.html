Points & playbook

Each clue you reveal costs points, so ration them and guess accurately. Wrong submissions and resets will dent your total, while a confident, early solution keeps your leaderboard standing strong.

  • Track the clue cost and remaining score in the Session Stats panel.
  • Record the rounded crack time as soon as you confirm the password—it is the vault digit.
  • Need an extra hint? Spend the points intentionally and note the deduction.

Password Puzzle — Quick Guide

Digit three comes from the rounded crack minutes. Pair the clues with the lab to collect it.

  1. Reveal clues sparingly and guess the weak password in Step 1. Fewer hints mean more points.
  2. Once you have the answer, enter it into the Crack Time Lab (Step 2) to generate the rounded minutes.
  3. Record that minute value as the vault digit and continue to the next challenge.
