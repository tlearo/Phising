Password Puzzle — Quick Guide

Digit three comes from the rounded crack minutes. Pair the clues with the lab to collect it.

  1. Reveal clues sparingly and guess the weak password in Step 1. Fewer hints mean more points.
  2. Once you have the answer, enter it into the Crack Time Lab (Step 2) to generate the rounded minutes.
  3. Record that minute value as the vault digit and continue to the next challenge.
